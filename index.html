Why We're Different

Authentic:  We lay everything out for you.  Our products are all naturally derived and scientifically proven.  All ingredients are listed on the product pages.

Affordable:  Our vow is to never have a product cost more than $30.00.  Brazen Babe’s products offer high quality skin care at budget prices.

Accessible:  All of our products are Paraben Free, Non-GMO, Vegan, Cruelty Free, Gluten Free, Made in the USA, GMP and Formulated with Sensitive Skin in mind.  We want everyone to be able to use and enjoy our products.  Brazen Babe is Guilt Free Skin Care.

We Make a Difference:  For every purchase $1 is donated to NWCAVE, the National Women’s Coalition Against Violence and Exploitation.  Helping women overcome adversity, achieve and prosper is a main mission of Brazen Babe.
